# Task 3: Monthly Charges Distribution Analysis

## Objective
To analyze the distribution of MonthlyCharges in the Telco Customer Churn dataset and visualize it using a histogram.

## Dataset Information
- Dataset Name: Telco Customer Churn Dataset
- Total Records: 7,043 Customer Records
- Attribute Analyzed: MonthlyCharges

## Tasks Performed
- Analyzed the MonthlyCharges column of the dataset.
- Created a histogram to visualize the distribution of monthly charges.
- Used appropriate intervals to understand the spread of monthly charges.
- Formatted the chart with descriptive titles and axis labels.
- Derived key statistics and insights from the distribution.

## Key Statistics
- Minimum Monthly Charge: $18.25
- Average Monthly Charge: $64.76
- Maximum Monthly Charge: $118.75

## Key Insights
- Monthly charges range from $18.25 to $118.75.
- Most customers are concentrated in the mid-to-high monthly charge ranges.
- The distribution shows varying pricing plans and customer service preferences.
